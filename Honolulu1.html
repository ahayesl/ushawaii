The State Capitol


Honolulu, O'ahu


Honolulu is the capitol of Hawai'i. It is also the largest city in the State.

Fun facts about Honolulu:
  1. Honolulu is the only city in the U.S. To have a royal palace. The 'Iolani Palace was home to Queen Lili'uoklani.
    'Iolani Palace had electric lighting before the white house!

  2. Honolulu is where President Barack Obama was born and raised

  3. O'ahu island color is Yellow
